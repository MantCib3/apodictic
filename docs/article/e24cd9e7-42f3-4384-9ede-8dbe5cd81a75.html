Investment groups call for exemption of passive income from US tax bill

June 25, 2025
  • Six investment groups representing sectors including fund managers, venture capital, and real estate called for an exemption of passive income from a US tax bill.
  • The investment groups argued that the tax bill would unfairly target foreign investors.
  • The exemption would help to attract foreign investment and promote economic growth.
